About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Maria Bruner and I am originally from LaSalle, Illinois. Before stepping into the world of web development, I attended Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology in Terre Haute, Indiana. There I played varsity volleyball for four years and graduated with a BS in Mechanical Engineering in 2020. While at Rose-Hulman I fell in love with writing code through some basic coding and robotics courses, but I was committed to finishing my engineering degree in four years so I did not pursue it further at the time.

I currently live in Indianapolis and I am a Quality and Carbon Design Engineer at Makuta, INC., a micromolding and Carbon 3D partnered company in Shelbyville, Indiana. While studying code in my free time and researching a career in web development, I discovered ElevenFifty Academy's Web Development Bootcamp. Taking this course has confirmed my passion for writing code. Since March of 2021, I have learned Javascript, HTML, CSS, and all of the necessary tools to begin a career in web development. My projects below are shown in chronological order, so you will hopefully be able to see how I have grown as a developer throughout the course. Thank you for checking out my portfolio and please feel free to contact me through the form below!
