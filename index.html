About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Maria Bruner and I am originally from LaSalle, Illinois. Before stepping into the world of web development, I attended Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology in Terre Haute, Indiana. There I played varsity volleyball for four years and graduated with a BS in Mechanical Engineering in 2020. While at Rose-Hulman I fell in love with writing code after learning Matlab and being exposed to Python and C through some basic coding and robotics courses. However, I was committed to finishing my engineering degree in four years so I did not pursue it further at the time.

I now live in Indianapolis where I am a Quality and Carbon Design Engineer at Makuta, INC., a micromolding and Carbon 3D partnered company. While studying code in my free time and researching a career in web development, I discovered ElevenFifty Academy's part time web development bootcamp. Taking this course confirmed my interest in code and turned it into a passion. Since March of 2021, I have devoted most of my time outside of work practicing Javascript, HTML, CSS, and all of the necessary tools to begin a career in web development. I am fortunate to have found my passion so early in my career and I cannot wait to continue to learn and grow as a developer. My projects below are shown in chronological order, so you will hopefully be able to see how my skills have improved throughout the course. Thank you for checking out my portfolio and please feel free to contact me through the form below!
